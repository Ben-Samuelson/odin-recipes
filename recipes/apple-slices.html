Apple Slices

Description

It's apple slices. It's like an apple if you sliced it.

Ingredients

  • One apple
  • Dish soap

Steps

  1. Put a small dot of dish soap on the apple
  2. Wash the apple in the sink
  3. With a paring knife carefully cut out the center of the apple from the top
  4. With a chef's knife cut the apple from the top into 6 slices
