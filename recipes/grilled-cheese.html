Grilled Cheese

Decription

This is a simple grilled cheese recipe you can make in about 15 minutes using common ingredients. I found this to be the perfect recipe to make if you're a college student with a kitchen who wants something more than cup ramen but you don't have the time to make something extravagant.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of white bread
  • One square of cheese of your choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Set both slices of bread on a plate by the stove
  2. (Optional) Lightly toast both slices of bread to more easily spread the butter
  3. Gently spread the butter thoroughly on both faces of both slices of bread
  4. Set the stove heat to 4 or 5 and place one of the bread slices in a pan on the stove
  5. Wait until the face-down side of the bread is a golden-brown color - check intermittently with a spatula
  6. Flip the bread in the pan and place the cheese square on top
  7. Add the second slice of bread to the pan
  8. Wait until the face-down sides of both slices of bread are the same golden-brown color as before - continuing to check with the spatula
  9. Flip the second slice of bread and put the first slice of bread on top of it so the cheese is between the two slices
  10. Wait until the face-down side of the sandwich is the same golden-brown color and continue checking with the spatula
  11. Turn off the heat and put the sandwich on a plate - you're done!
