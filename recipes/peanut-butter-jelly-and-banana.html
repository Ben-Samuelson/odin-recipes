PBJ and Banana

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic lunch food, but being a classic means its rather mundane. If you like peanut butter and jelly but if you want to mix it up, a little banana is the perfect touch!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut butter or a suitable alternative
  • Jelly of your choose of fruit
  • One banana

Steps

  1. Set both slices of bread on a plate
  2. With a butter knife, spread the jelly on the face-up side of one of the slices
  3. Wipe the jelly off the knife on the face-up side of the second slice of bread
  4. Spread the peanut butter on the face-up side of the second slice of bread
  5. Peel the banana
  6. Use the knife to cut thin slices of banana and place them on the second slice of bread until the surface is covered
  7. Place the first slice of bread face down on the second slice so the peanut butter and jelly are both on the inside and the banana slices are in the center
  8. (Optional) Gently cut the sandwich in half - cut with horizontal motions and don't press down
